Taco Recipe

Description

This beef taco recipe will be an excellent addition to your weekly meals. They are also very customisable for your high protein, low fat, low carb, low GI, etc needs! It only uses a few simple ingredients, most of which are probably sitting in your pantry! So what are you waiting for!? Get to it!

Ingredients

    Taco Meat

  • 500g of mince beef
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp corn starch
  • salt and pepper

    • Pico de Gallo

  • 1 tomato
  • 3 avocados
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 3 limes

    • Toppings

  • Cheddar or Mexican-style cheese
  • Sour cream
