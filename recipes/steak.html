Steak Recipe

this is not my steak it is someone else's

Description

This steak recipe will teach you how to make a probably delicious steak! I ensure you it will be delicious because I am very trustworthy

Ingredients

    The steak

  • The steak
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • A bundle of thyme

    • The mushroom sauce

  • The mushrooms
  • 400 ml cream
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • Parsley
  • Cooking oil
  • Cooking wine optional

    • Other essentials

  • salt and pepper
  • a meat thermometer it is literally so important pls

Steps

    Making the mushroom sauce

  1. Add butter and your chosen cooking oil to a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add your garlic and onions, saute them until brown. Then add in the mushrooms, cook them for 6-7 minutes to get that water OUT!.
  3. Once the water has mostly evaporated and your mushrooms have shrunken, add some seasoning.
  4. Add in all your cream and stir continuously until it thickens.
  5. Season to taste and add in chopped parsley.

    6. Cooking the steak

  6. Salt your steak before cooking it and make sure the surface of it is dry.
  7. Add oil to a high heat pan. The steak should make a sizzling noise when placed on the pan.
  8. Cook the steak on both sides until nice and crispy, after 2-3 minutes add in butter and thyme and begin to butter baste it.
  9. Stop once it reaches 55-60 degrees Celsius internal for medium rare. Allow the steak to rest for 2-3 minutes before serving.

    10. The last bit

  10. Serve your steak with a nice amount of mushroom sauce.
  11. Consider adding sides like chips, salad, coleslaw, or a sexy glass of wine.
